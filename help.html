How to join the fight! 6 Steps

#1 Get Involved! Be Involved!

Getting and being involved with your communities climate change events (ex: fundraising, online charity). Also in a political view, voting for your next politician that has a good understanding and willing to help and change and help the climate. With the right politician helps, with a bigger voice and bigger movement.

#2 Energy Efficient = Better World

Having some many electronics plugged in, either it's charging or its just on and it's collecting dust, might as well just leaving it off. Also when washing your clothes, try not using hot water. You can change by breaking those habits and will help climate change.

#3 What you are eating, Factors in!

There's many ways to help the climate change from the food you are eating. You can buy organic food and locally grown food, instead of processed food.

#4 Renewable Power

If your current house is able to switch to renewable power, will help alot. Such as wind power, solar energy products, geothmeral.

#5 Re-use Re-cycle!

Recycling still uses less energy than making new products from scratch, but reducing and reusing are even cleaner. So next time you have something to throw, check if you can recycle it!

#6 Transportation

Cars, busses, planes! Still a major factor on climate change! To reduce climate change, leave your car at home and take the bus, atleast its one transportation. Or better yet take your bike.
